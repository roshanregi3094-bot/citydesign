About The City Design

City Design is a Kannur-based trading and construction company with over 20 years of industry experience, delivering reliable and high-quality projects since 2005. With expertise spanning residential, commercial, institutional, and high-rise developments, we have successfully executed projects across Kerala and beyond. Driven by precision, integrity, and long-term value, City Design stands as a trusted name in modern construction.
